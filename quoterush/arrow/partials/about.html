About Arrow Quotes

Since its launch in late 2012, Arrow has become one of the most popular hero series on air. Based on DC’s Green Arrow, this series follows Oliver Queen as he becomes the Arrow and fights evil in Starling City.

Arrow’s strong actors, gripping story and awesome superhero moves have made it popular all over the world. With its first season already averaging millions of views per episode, it has already created it’s own spinoff and won multiple awards.

Through our app, you will relive the experience through the words of Arrow, Felicity Smoak, Black Canary, Deathstroke, John Diggle, and even The Flash
